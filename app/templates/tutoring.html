Tutoring Services

Due to the spread of COVID-19 across New York City and the rest of the United States:

  • Many colleges, universities, and school districts have closed for unprecendented periods of time.
  • Students are therefore required to switch to distance learning.
  • Everyone is also expected to practice social distancing, which may be especially stressful for students.
  • This transition is likely to accompanied by several difficulties as students and instructors migrate to a virtual platform.
  • As a result, students will not be able to receive the same quality of learning online as they would in a classroom.
  • The problems students now face are especially detrimental to students from underserved districts.

Because of this, I am advertising my tutoring services for these students in need.

I have years of experience tutoring mostly college students, but I am also comfortable working with high school students as well.

  1. I will dedicate at least three to five hours of free tutoring per week (one-on-one or group tutoring, depending on demand) to
    • Underserved students
    • Students from low-income backgrounds (annual family income of less than $60,000)
    • Students underrepresented in STEM backgrounds
  2. For other students, I will charge only $20/hour to accommodate the difficulties students may face transitioning to an online medium.
    • Note: If you are a student who I am currently TA-ing or tutoring within Stony Brook University's Academic Success and Tutoring Center, I cannot tutor you privately as this would represent a conflict of interest.
    • Disclaimer: I will not work on assignments that will be submitted for a grade. I can help with similar or previously graded problems, but I will not solve homework or exam problems, nor will I write essays for tutees. I am also not a consultant or adviser. Ultimately, each student is responsible for their own learning.
  3. To facilitate social distancing, all tutoring sessions will be given via videoconferencing (Skype, Facebook, Discord, Hangouts, etc).

For more information about me and my qualifications, please see the "About" page.
