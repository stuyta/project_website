About

My name is Shivasuryan Vummidi and I am currently a sophomore at Stony Brook University's Honors College pursuing a BS in Biochemistry on the pre-medical track, with minors in Chemistry and Writing & Rhetoric. I graduated Stuyvesant High School in 2018 with an Advanced Regents Diploma.


Aside from science classes in college, I have a very strong passion for writing. Besides typical academic papers, I have been writing poems, short stories, and research-style prose since my junior year of high school. Writing serves as my main creative outlet and this website allows me to share my thoughts with the global community on an open platform.


Creating this website also helped rekindle my old passion for computer science, and I am excited to pursue new projects and learn new computer languages.


I also enjoy tutoring students - tutoring allows me to make a difference and touch the lives of students from a mentor's point of view. Therefore, I have created this website in mind of both realizing my passion and serving students in need.
